Overview

Summertide Resort is family and pet friendly and offers vacation rentals and RV spots near the beautiful Hood Canal, an inlet of the Puget Sound in Washington. We offer our guests a relaxed and stress-free environment so you can enjoy a quiet vacation with friends and family. During your Washington vacation on the Hood Canal, our rentals and RV spots will accommodate all of your lodging needs. Enjoy having full kitchen facilities along with access to our convenience store. Whether you stay in a rental or RV spot, you'll find all the comforts and conveniences needed to complete your vacation on the Hood Canal.

Our marina on Hood Canal offers tons of exciting activities for everyone to enjoy. Summertide offers 240 feet of waterfront so spend your vacation in Tahuya with non-stop activities on Hood Canal including boating, swimming, fishing, crabbing and shrimping in season, as well as our campfire and Friday night cookouts. We also have boat rentals and kayak rentals as well as a boat launch and parking for boat trailers so you can fully enjoy all that Hood Canal has to offer. Don't forget other fun activities such as bird-watching as well as storm watching lures some to our private beach and tidal life viewing. Let the Summertide Resort take your vacation to the next level.

Start planning your vacation to Tahuya, Washington and the Hood Canal and reserve one of our rentals or RV spots today! At the Summertide Resort and Marina, we offer a warm and friendly resort to be your home away from home.

The moorage docks are put in before May 1st and removed sometime in September. There are 22 inside moorage slips that can take up to a 20' boat. There are several outside slips that can handle much larger boats. We have annual moorage and nightly moorage. We do not allow guest to sleep aboard their boats. RV Park

Each RV site in our private campgrounds has full hookups — water, power, septic, cable, and garbage.

Join our RV Beach Club: Many of our RV spaces are rented by the month with the tenants leaving their trailer here and using our Resort as their waterfront home.

We are located near five golf courses and there are several hiking and public forest parks in the area. Crabbing is a favorite sport for our guests, and we have crab pots that can be rented during crab season. We sell fishing and shellfish licenses here at the resort. We have a small grocery store, well stocked with all the essentials including beer and wine. The closest large grocery store is located in Belfair. Reservations are recommend during peak season, weekends, and holidays.

Marina

SummerTide boat launch is open to the public year around. A single launch is $12.00 or buy an annual launch pass for $100. Maximum size boat that can easily be launched is 24 feet and that would depend on your vehicle and trailer length. Boats can be launched up to a zero tide. Best to check the tide chart before you come out! The moorage docks are put in before May 1st and removed sometime in September.

There are 22 inside moorage slips that can take up to a 20' boat. There are several outside slips that can handle much larger boats. We have annual moorage and nightly moorage. We do not allow guest to sleep aboard their boats. Nightly moorage for non-guest is based on availability. Reservations are required. Appropriate ropes and buoy's are required, you will be asked to read and follow written rules available on-site in the office.
